ABOUT US

A Model United Nations Conference(MUN) is a simulation of international committees wherein national and international issues are tackled and talked about by students each of whom represents a country in the conference. MUN helps students develop their verbal and written English. It also improves debating, public-speaking and problem-solving skills. MUN conferences raise the youth's awareness of global problems and introduce them to people from different backgrounds. Another aim of MUN is to encourage students to share different views and try to understand these views.

St. Edmunds, Malviya Nagar would be hosting the second edition of its Model United Nations conference from 12th to 13th of November 2016. This year we'll be simulating four committees wherein we will discuss issues from around the world, also this year Edmund's will witness the simulation of a special committee called the 'Historic Lok Sabha' which is being conducted for the first time in the Rajasthan MUN/YP circuit.

MESSAGE FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Dear Delegates,

It gives me immense pleasure in welcoming you all to Edmund's MUN 2016, and to avow how gratifying I feel for having the opportunity to be the secretary general of its second edition. On behalf of my entire team I extend to you all a very warm welcome to Edmunds MUN 2016.

The motto behind Edmund's MUN is to bring out the leaders, the policy makers within us for a better tomorrow and like our tagline says "Debating for a better world" we believe in debate that leads towards a better world with better leaders and policies. This year we'll be simulating 4 committees wherein we'll witness discussions between delegates from across the country; about the measures of peace and security in the nation and around the world, UDHR and issues related to colonial conflicts. Also this year Edmund's will witness the simulation of a special committee called the 'Historic Lok Sabha' which is being conducted for the first time in the Rajasthan MUN/YP circuit.

Along with the conference there will be a lot for you to see in the historic and beautiful pink city, Jaipur, famous for its Rajasthani culture, delicious cuisines and magnificent forts which tell the tales of their great rulers. So put on your diplomatic attire, analyse the facts, formulate theories and let's make every possible contribution in our capacity towards making a better world.

As the famous quote says, "Every morning in Africa, a gazelle wakes up, it knows it must outrun the fastest lion or it will be killed. Every morning in Africa, a lion wakes up. It knows it must run faster than the slowest gazelle, or it will starve. It doesn't matter whether you're the lion or a gazelle; when the sun comes up, you'd better be running."



Ayush Rawtani
Secretary General
